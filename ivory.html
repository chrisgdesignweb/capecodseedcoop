Seed Keepers: Bill Braun and Deanna Levanti

Bill Braun and Deanna Levanti grow vegetables, flowers, herbs, fruit, and seeds with great respect for biodiversity at Ivory Silo Farm in Westport, MA. In 2017, they founded Freed Seed Federation, a farmer-run 501c3 nonprofit dedicated to the preservation, improvement, and diversification of place-based, climate-resilient seed for the public commons. Freed Seed Federation also educates farmers on seed keeping, and partners them with plant breeders in an effort to restore the farmer's role as participant, rather than recipient, in the plant breeding process.

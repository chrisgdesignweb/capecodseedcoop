Ida Gold

One of the earlies tomatoes I've grown here on Cape Cod. Its a yellow bush semi-determinate tomato that is a big bigger in size than a cherry. I've been saving seeds from these for a few years and selecting for earliness and fruit size. This heirloom was originally bred by the University of Idaho specifically for northern growers.

Vilms Paste

Just one of the best and hardiest paste tomatoes I've grown here on Cape Cod. Indeterminate. It can produce alot of fruit in the right growing season, its mostly a plum shaped tomato. The origin of the tomato is not known for certain but it is possibly Lithuanian in origin - from Seed Savers Exchange. I originally got this seed from the FEDCO Coop in Maine.

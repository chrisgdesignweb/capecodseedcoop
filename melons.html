Crane Melon

This is one of the most successful melons that I have grown here on Cape Cod. It is part of the Slow Food Ark of Taste and has fantastic flavor. Oliver Crane developed the Crane Melon in the early 1900s by crossing several varieties of melons, including a Japanese melon, a white melon, a Persian melon and an ambrosia melon among others.

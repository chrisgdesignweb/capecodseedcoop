Growing Seeds for the Future of Cape Cod

Cape Cod Seed Coop exists to help create a resilient seed and food system in our region. We connect gardeners and growers to help adapt crops to our ever changing climate here on Cape Cod. Money is not exchanged here, but ideas and seeds are.

Our Seeds

We are currently holding a small collection of vegetable and flower seed that are available for Seed Keepers on Cape Cod. This Open Pollinated collection is growing and we hope to increase it over the next couple of years.

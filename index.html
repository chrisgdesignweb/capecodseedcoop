Seeds in the Commons for Gardens of Cape Cod

Seeds do not belong in the hands of multi national corporations but in the hands of gardeners. Cape Cod is a resiliant place and its people are as well. With our climate changing rapidly, we will need our crops to as well. Our seeds need resiliance and climate adaptability, join in today and become a Seed Keeper of Cape Cod.

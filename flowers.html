Gillis Sparky Marigold

These merigolds bloom different shades of yellow, gold and red on large 2 ft plants. Great for pest management in an organic system. I've been selecting this over the years for color as well as size in the garden. Super hardy and will last until the final frost. The origins of the seed come from a Darold Decker intro from the late 1950s.
